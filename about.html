About Us

Shirdi Steel Re-Rollers also known as SSR, started operations in Goa in the year 1998 at Cuncolim Industrial Estate as Manufacturing division of SAI BABA brand steel. It is one of the renowned brand, manufacturers of high tensile strength mild structural Steel such as Angles, Channels, Square Bars, Flats and Beams.
SSR is Quality conscious organ ization backed up by a capacious and advanced infrastructure, effective after sales support and various clients across India. Marketed and Promoted in all the neighboring states such as Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil-Nadu, Andhra-Pradesh, Maharashtra and Goa. Our goal is to become a global leader in quality Steel Manufacturing. We strive to implement the best technologies to improve quality & productivity. We streamline our supply chain in order to deliver value added products to our customers promptly.

What do we do?

Sponge Iron Facility: Sponge iron is derived out of best quality iron ore sourced from the Bellary/ Hospet regions of Karnataka best known for their high fe content crucial to sponge iron making.
Billet Making Facility: Billets are produced from secondary steel making facility with innovative technology in tune with modern steel making practices and techniques.
Shirdi Steel has commissioned a state of the art continuous medium and light structural mill with advanced rolling mill technology and equipment to manufacture sections catering to different and diverse segments of industry.
